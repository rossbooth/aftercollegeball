Q: How many D1 players were analyzed?
A: We analyzed 18,688 D1 players whose last college season was between 2014-15 and 2024-25. Of these, 816 (4.4%) reached the NBA, 1,065 (5.7%) went to the G-League, 2,073 (11.1%) played in Europe, 3,168 (17.0%) played in other international leagues, and 11,566 (61.9%) had no recorded pro career.

Q: What percentage of D1 players go pro?
A: 38.1% of D1 players in our dataset went on to play some form of professional basketball. Only 4.4% made the NBA with real regular-season playing time. 5.7% went to the G-League, 11.1% to Europe, and 17.0% to other international leagues.
